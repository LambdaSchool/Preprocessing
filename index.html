Who I am

I am the official ruler of Quadling County in the northern quadrant of Oz. I have ruled over and protected this county ever since the overthrow of the Wicked Witch of the South. I live in a palace near the southern border of the Quadling Country, attended by fifty beautiful maidens from each country of Oz. I am strongly protective of her subjects in the South. I have created a walled, gated communities for the rabbits of Bunnybury and the paper dolls of Miss Cuttenclip, showing a personal interest in the concerns of not only the humanoid Quadlings, but also the other inhabitants of my jurisdiction. I am experienced and seasoned to the point of knowing that there is not a magic cure for everything, and that certain things cannot be changed or perhaps should not be changed for better or for worse.

  • Owner of the Great Book of Records
  • Advocate and protector of the Munchkins
  • CEO of the Female Soldier's Army
  • Advocate for shape the future of the Land of Oz as a whole
