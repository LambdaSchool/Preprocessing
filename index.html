About

I'm Natsu the brother of Zeref Dragneel, who was born 400 years before the start of Fairy Tail. When I was young, My parents were killed by a dragon. Desperate to get them back, my brother Zeref researched dark magic and resurrected me as a demon known as E.N.D. From then on, I was raised by Igneel, the Fire Dragon King, who taught me how to read & write various programming languages but enough about me keep scrolling to check out some of the stuff I've accomplished.
