About My Laser

The real reason you're here is because you want to know more about the laser. It's a 60kW-class fiber laser, mounted to my head by an exceptionally thin polyacrilonitrile nanofiber, a type of synthetic polymer related to acrylic, using a technique called electrospinning. The weapon itself is a beam-combined fiber laser, meaning different laser modules are fired up and combined using a fiber optic cable into a single light source.
