About Me

Hey there! My name is Tamantha Slate and I love making people look good. I am a makeup artist, photographer, and youtuber. It was at the age of 17 when I discovered my passion for makeup and photography. My siblings designated me as the makeup artist whenever it was time for their proms. I loved creating the look they asked me for.

Whenever a client smiles at their reflection in the mirror, I smile as well. My mission in life is to experience that joy each and every day!
