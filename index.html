About me

My name is Jack Skellington. There are few who'd deny, at what I do I am the best. My talents are renowned far and wide. When it comes to surprises in the moonlit night, I excel without ever even trying.

With the slightest little effort of my ghostlike charms, I have seen grown men give out a shriek! With a wave of my hand, and a well placed moan I have sweopt the very bravest off their feet!
